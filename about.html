About ScentGuide PK

ScentGuide PK publishes practical perfume content for shoppers in Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, and nationwide delivery across Pakistan.

What we publish

We combine wear tests, longevity notes in heat and humidity, and transparent discussion of authorised versus grey-market channels commonly used in Pakistan. Our goal is to reduce expensive mistakes—whether you chase designer classics or Arabian value houses.

Transparency

Future monetisation might include affiliate links (for example marketplace partners serving Pakistan). When that happens, we will disclose it near outbound purchase links so readers understand how we earn. Editorial picks are never purchased rankings.

Work with us

For corrections, collaborations, or press samples, reach out via our contact page.
